Welcome to Maharashtra Mandal Finland

Maharashtra Mandal Finland is a registered organization that consists of all the members from the state of ‘Maharashtra’ living in Finland. Started with an objective to come together on a small scale recently the no of FB joined members has increased to around 210 members living in and around the capital city of Helsinki. The main objective of MM Finland is ‘To bring and blossom together the Maharashtrian community by celebrating all the events together in a traditional way and making the kids aware of our cultural and traditional values.’ Every member is welcome to register and take advantage of one roof information and being part of a big social group.

About Us

Maharashtra Mandal Finland Ry is a cultural non-profit making organisation with the main purpose of nurturing and preserving the rich culture and traditions of Maharashtra. It warmly welcomes Citizens of India and Finland who wish to celebrate the rich culture and heritage of Maharashtra. The regional language of state of Maharashtra is mainly ‘Marathi’. It lies in western region of India and rightly called the ‘Land of Rocks’ as it preserves the ancient historically significant forts and caves. The Sahyadri Mountain ranges are the backbone that have been protecting the western region for ages exhibiting the magnificent naturistic beauty attracting tourists from all over the world.

This organisation has been formed with a mission to

  • Organise Cultural Events and provide a forum to promote, preserve, nurture and share the Heritage and Culture of Maharashtra
  • Familiarise next generations with the traditions and cultures of Maharashtra
  • Help newly arriving people with the required informative Assistance, if any.
  • Provide platform for the local talents in various field to exhibit.
  • Conduct different kinds of workshops for suitable ages that nurture the essence of Maharashtra, India
  • Arrange 4-5 Cultural Get Together that celebrate the main festivals of Maharashtra
  • Though the main Language of Communication is Marathi, the main parts will also be included in English for the Non-Marathi Members who want to be the part of community to celebrate the rich culture of Maharashtra.
